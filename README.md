## Core Automations Every Florida Local Business Should Have

**1. Missed Call Text-Back**
Someone calls, you can't answer — an AI texts them instantly so you never lose the lead.

**2. Lead Follow-Up Sequences**
New enquiry comes in — automated messages go out within 60 seconds across SMS, email, and voicemail.

**3. Appointment Booking**
Let customers book themselves. No back and forth. Calendar fills automatically.

**4. Review Generation**
After every job, automatically ask happy customers for a Google review.

**5. AI Receptionist**
24/7 chat or voice agent that qualifies leads and books appointments while you sleep.
